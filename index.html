WHAT IS IEDC?

IEDC (Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Center) is a student-driven initiative that nurtures creativity, entrepreneurship, and innovation among young minds. It acts as a launchpad for new ideas, encouraging students to turn problems into opportunities through real-time tech and business solutions.
